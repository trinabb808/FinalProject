Information about Accessibility and Web Design

Web accessibility is important for websites so that people who have disabilities can use them with ease. An accessible website has many benefits for individuals, businesses, and society. It can help businesses reach a broader audience because it makes it easier for more people to access their content. Overall, accessible websites provide a better experience for all users.
